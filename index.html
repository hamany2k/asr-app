Bloom

A goal-tracking app that treats progress like a garden. Set one goal, work it in small steps, and watch it grow — literally — instead of staring at a checklist that never moves.

Business model: Freemium iOS app. Free tier includes one active goal with unlimited plan regeneration; Bloom Pro (subscription via StoreKit 2) unlocks additional goals. No ads, no data resale.

ASR Coffee

A private-label coffee roast built around Ethiopia Yirgacheffe, sourced directly and packaged for people who want one honest cup — no subscriptions, no gimmicks.

Business model: Direct-to-consumer retail via asrkw.com, with capsule and whole-bean SKUs. Sourced from small suppliers in Kuwait, Hungary, and Switzerland; first release targeted for Q4 2026.
